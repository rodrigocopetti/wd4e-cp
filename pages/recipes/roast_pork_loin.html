Roast pork loin

Surprise everyone with this simply irresistible roast pork loin recipe! Your lunch or dinner will turn into a true gastronomic celebration.

Roast pork loin is a classic and irresistible option for family gatherings, especially during festive occasions like Christmas. This traditional recipe brings a juicy and flavorful loin, ensuring a main dish that will delight all your guests.

With a simple yet powerful marinade made of garlic, onion, bay leaves, lemon juice, olive oil, and salt, the pork absorbs the flavors and aromas, resulting in tender and tasty meat. Roasting in the oven completes the process, creating a golden crust on the outside and preserving juiciness inside.

This dish is perfect to serve with classic Christmas sides such as farofa, Greek rice, or roasted potatoes.

Follow these simple steps and earn compliments with a delicious and irresistible roast pork loin at your festive table!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 kg of pork loin
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 4 bay leaves
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, in pieces
  • 2 tablespoons of lemon juice
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of salt
